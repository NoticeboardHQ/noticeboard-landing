Privacy Policy for Noticeboard Mobile App
Last Updated: 27/02/2024

1. Introduction Welcome to Noticeboard! This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled with care and diligence. 2. User Privacy At Noticeboard, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users' personal data. We comply with applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for the European Union, Personal Information Protection Law, Identification Method for Illegal Collection, and Provisions on the Cyber Protection of Children's Personal Information for the Chinese mainland, and Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance for Hong Kong (China), Personal Data Protection Act for Singapore, as well as other relevant local privacy laws. 3. Privacy Policies 3.1 Easy Access to Privacy Policy: Our app provides an easy-to-find in-app link to our privacy policy, which contains consistent and transparent information about the processing of personal data. 3.2 Comprehensive Privacy Policy: Our privacy policy includes the purpose, method, and scope of personal data collection and use by our app, including any third parties involved. 3.3 User Rights: We inform users about how they can exercise their data subject rights, such as withdrawing consent, requesting data deletion, and accessing their personal data. We also provide an effective channel for user privacy issue feedback. 3.4 Account Deregistration: Users can easily deregister their accounts without any obstacles, and the deregistration takes effect promptly. 4. Obtaining User Consent 4.1 Clear Information: We provide clear access to our privacy policy and relevant information before requesting consent for collecting or processing personal data. 4.2 Withdrawal of Consent: Users can easily withdraw their consent at any time through a simple and understandable process. 4.3 Personalized Advertising: If we collect and use personal data for personalized advertising and marketing, users are informed, and they have the option to disable this function. 5. Collection and Use 5.1 Minimization Principle: We adhere to the principle of minimization, collecting and using user data only to the extent necessary to achieve the intended purpose. 5.2 Transparency: We do not collect or use personal data in a secretive, excessive, or unreasonable manner. 5.3 Security: User data is processed securely using encryption technologies such as HTTPS. 5.4 Disclosure: If personal data is disclosed, we specify the data, recipient, and purpose in our privacy policy. 5.5 No Sale of Personal Data: We do not sell any personal data to third parties. 6. Permission Requests 6.1 Minimization: We request permissions based on the minimization principle and do not deny services based on consent. 6.2 Location Permission: We do not request location permission for advertising or data analysis purposes only. 6.3 Clear Description: We provide clear descriptions of functions and scenarios that request permissions and do not induce or mislead users' authorization.
