Dashboard Features

View and manage building communications from a single place. Arrange communications based on serverity level, so you can appropriately prioritise them.

Manage tenant complaints

View, manage and settle complaints against tenants, before they escalate futher. Provide your tenants with a free to use mobile app, that allows them to collect and organise the evidence of each complaint, so you can escalate easier if you need to.

Manage security staff

Get a comprehensive breakdown of which security staff is on shift, whether tenants are satisfied with them, and how long they take to respond to complaints. Assign tasks to security staff with the click of a button.

Send building-wide alerts

Instantly let tenants know the next time there's a water or electricity outage in the building, with the click of a button.
